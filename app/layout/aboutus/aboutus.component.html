About Us


SenRa, a contributing member of the LoRa Alliance TM, is a Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN) provider for IoT/M2M applications. We provide long distance communication for LoRa enabled devices leveraging open standards architechture of the global LoRa Alliance TM. Our connectivity services are available at fractional costs of competing technologies such as cellular, Wi-Fi, or satellite net-works.
